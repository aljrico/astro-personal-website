*Last updated: May 2026*

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Test DGT collects, how it is used, and what stays on your device.

## Data We Collect

### Study Data

- Answers, mistakes, completed questions, topic progress, exam attempts, streaks, and practice history
- This data is stored locally on your device
- We do not sell your study data

### Purchase Data

- Premium Pack purchases are managed through the App Store
- RevenueCat is used to verify whether the Premium Pack has been purchased
- We do not receive or store your payment card details

### Advertising Data

- The free version may show ads through Google AdMob
- AdMob may process identifiers and device information to deliver, limit, measure, and improve ads
- Where required, the app asks for consent before using personalized advertising

### Basic Analytics

- Anonymous or aggregated usage information may be used to improve app quality, diagnose issues, and understand feature usage
- We do not use this data to identify you personally

## Local Storage

Your exam progress and practice history are stored on your device. If you uninstall the app, local study data may be deleted.

## Third Parties

- **Apple** — App Store downloads, purchases, refunds, and payment processing
- **RevenueCat** — Premium Pack purchase verification
- **Google AdMob** — advertising and ad measurement

Each provider handles data according to its own privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy

Test DGT is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
