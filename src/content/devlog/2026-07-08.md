Pure polish day. Four commits, zero features, and I'd do it again.

The arena header was scrolling away with the rest of the board and it bothered me more than it should. So: frosted panel. Back button, league name, season countdown, trophy rail, all floating on white at 72% with a soft shadow while the leaderboard slides underneath. The moment it clicked into place I just sat there scrolling up and down for a minute. This is the stuff.

Then the league name. It was polite little Nunito and the arena deserves a poster. ALL CAPS, Barlow SemiCondensed, weight 900, chocolate red. The size hunt was pure hot-reload loop: 21, no. 25, closer. 30, nice. 40, way too much, it looked like a boxing promo. Back to 30. Edit, signal the running app, look, nudge. No thinking, just eyes. That loop is the closest thing I have to painting.

One nice catch mid-flow: I wanted the material word (Wood, Bronze, whatever) at full brightness and the word "League" dimmed. First version was a whole token classifier, per-word logic, very clever. Then I looked at it and thought: it's one regex. Match the league word and its connector, dim that, done. Covers English, Spanish, Portuguese, Catalan, even the Dutch compound. The clever version went in the bin and nothing of value was lost. Code quality is a story we tell ourselves; the regex ships.

Second commit: the promotion and demotion zones had these thick green and red borders around the rows and they were shouting. Moved the color into the rank numeral instead. Sage for going up, watermelon for going down. Quieter, better. At one point I asked for "redHeart" from the palette with total confidence. There is no redHeart. There has never been a redHeart. I invented a color, got attached to it, and had to be shown the actual options like a child at a paint store.

And then the one that actually means something: the senyera. If your device says Spain but your language says Catalan, the leaderboard now flies the senyera instead of the Spanish flag. Device region plus language code, ES-CT on the profile, one SVG copied into the flags folder, old app versions just show nothing instead of crashing. Even the bots mirror it so the leagues don't look segregated.

Tiny feature. Handful of lines. But I know exactly how it feels to open a leaderboard and see the flag that isn't quite yours. Some Catalan kid is going to open the arena and see the senyera next to their name and feel *seen*, and no A/B test is ever going to measure that. The physicist brain wants to ask whether any of today moved retention. The rest of me knows the board looks right now, and that it didn't yesterday.

Good day. Venga, next one.
